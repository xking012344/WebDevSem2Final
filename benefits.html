Why you should enroll for the entrepreneurship program

Since you may be wondering why you should enroll for the entrepreneurship program here at WCHS, here are some things that make enrolling in the program worth it and beneficial to your education and even your future after high school
(These are all things students were able to do in the 2023 - 2024 school year so just imagine what you'll be able to do)

  • Take field trips to places like P30 here on the Eastside and Gainbridge Fieldhouse to compete in an innovative workshop
  • You will have several opportunities to promote business ideas on local news channels
  • Pitch you ideas to real investors who are able to make offers or suggestions towards your product
  • Interact with several influencers, celebrities, and small business owners to get better experience
  • Compete with thousands of students around the state for seed money toward your business ideas
  • Participate in a financial podcast through the WTHR television channel
  • One or more mentorship with professionals
